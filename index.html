Trainings

Plumbing

Plumbing installs, repairs, and maintains fluid systems in buildings for water and gas. It includes pipes, fixtures, and appliances

House Wiring

House wiring installs and maintains electrical systems for safe power supply. It covers wiring, outlets, and fixtures in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

Motor Rewinding

Motor rewinding is the process of replacing or repairing the windings in an electric motor to restore its functionality.

3 Phase industrial Wiring

3-phase industrial wiring installs and maintains electrical systems for efficient power distribution in industrial settings. It involves wiring, equipment connections, and fixtures.

AC Repairing

Air conditioner repair involves diagnosing and fixing issues within the system to restore proper functioning and maintain optimal cooling performance.

Fridge and Washing Machine

Fridge and washing machine repairing involves diagnosing and fixing issues in these appliances. Technicians address problems like malfunctions, leaks, or faulty components.
